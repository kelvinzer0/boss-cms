# Help Center

Welcome to the Birds of Space Help Center! Here, you can find information on how to get in touch with us regarding partnerships, listings, bug reports, or other inquiries. We’re here to help!

---

## Partnership Opportunities

Interested in collaborating with Birds of Space? Whether you’re looking for a strategic partnership, sponsorship, or joint venture, we’d love to hear from you.

- **Email**: [partnerships@birdsofspace.com](mailto:partnerships@birdsofspace.com)
- **What to Include**:
  - Your name and company/organization details.
  - A brief description of your proposed partnership idea.
  - Any relevant links or supporting documents.

We’ll review your request and get back to you as soon as possible.

---

## Listing Requests

If you’d like to list your product, service, or event on our platform, please follow the steps below:

- **Email**: [listings@birdsofspace.com](mailto:listings@birdsofspace.com)
- **What to Include**:
  - Your name and contact information.
  - Details about what you’d like to list (product, service, event, etc.).
  - Links to your website or other relevant platforms.
  - Any specific requirements or deadlines.

Our team will evaluate your request and respond promptly.

---

## Bug Reports

Found a bug or technical issue on our platform? Let us know so we can fix it as soon as possible.

- **Email**: [bugs@birdsofspace.com](mailto:bugs@birdsofspace.com)
- **What to Include**:
  - A clear description of the issue.
  - Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable).
  - Screenshots or error messages (if available).
  - Your device and browser information (e.g., iPhone 12, Chrome Browser).

We appreciate your help in improving our platform!

---

## General Inquiries

For all other questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our general support team:

- **Email**: [info@birdsofspace.com](mailto:info@birdsofspace.com)

---

Thank you for reaching out to Birds of Space. We look forward to assisting you!
